About The Page

  Magico de Lumad is a one-of-a-kind e-commerce website where every product is inspired by Lumad crafts and ethnic patterns. Every item, whether it’s a bag, wallet, vase or placemat, is meticulously designed to reflect a traditional look and feel while maintaining modern functionality. This website also sells home decor items like chairs and basket. A perfect fit for your stylish life.Its purpose is to helps promote our locally produce products, especially the Tribe products in our country.

It also has a feature where our website accepts students who are willing to enroll in our workshops. The workshop divides into different kinds of teaching, such as weaving ethnic patterns, stitching bags, and how to make beautiful handicrafts.
