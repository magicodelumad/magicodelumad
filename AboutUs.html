Our Mission

Our mission is to help local products involving Lumad's work of arts, to be well-known around the globe. We strive to offer ourcustomers the lowest possible prices, the best available selection, the best possible service, and give them an experience that will keep them coming back.

Our Vision

Make commerce more user-friendly so that businesses may focus on what they do best: creating and selling their products. To make life better for many people, revive culture and support local businesses.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to use technology to make a positive difference in the world. We want to provide a platform where buyers and sellers can connect and help the community grow.

Our Personality

Our company's success is one that we are constantly creating, developing, and improving. Our personality is defined by values such as simplicity, positivity, and compassion. These values are reflected in everything we do, from the products we create to the way we collaborate with one another and with our customers

Meet the Team

Hello! We are the students of Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela taking Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, and we currently developed an E-commerce website that will sell products inspired by a traditional Lumad crafts and ethnic patterns. This is a small business that aim to promote local products. We do hope that you will be interested with our products and support our small business. Thank you very much!

John Paul Olea

Web Developer

Hello, I'm a 2nd year BSIT student and one of the website's developers. I hope you enjoy this e-commerce site and are motivated and inspired to purchase lumad crafts to help revive our Philippine native arts. Supporting local businesses that sell Native fashion and arts, in particular, can also go a long way toward reviving our long-forgotten culture..

Izabella Marie Quitil

Web Content Writer

I consider, write about, and initiate a discussion regarding our Philippine native arts. Bringing it back to reality and understanding its significance in our culture. This ecommerce website not only allows you to buy and sell lumad crafts, but it also helps local businesses promote our out-of-style fashions. Let us support local businesses since every small purchase matters.

Kayrellene Rellama

Web Designer

This website was created with Lumad patterns in sight. I utilize a color scheme that is based on aspects of Indigenous people, particularly lumad, the color of bravery and pride. They continue to defy all odds as they string beads and weave accessories that express their pride as a bright and out-of-the-box tribe through their fashion and culture-perfected beadwork. Philippine traditional arts must be revived.
